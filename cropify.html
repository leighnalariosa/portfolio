Cropify

Empowering Hawai'i's food independence through local solutions for a stronger, healthier tomorrow.

This mobile app is joining the larger movement to reduce Hawai'i's reliance on imported food. An overwhelming 85-90% of the food consumed in the state is imported, resulting in a compromise in both food quality and nutritional value due to the transportation process. Our reliance on external resources for food place us in a precarious position and exposes us to potential vulnerabilities.
